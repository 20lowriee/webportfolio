Usability Testing

Method

The website I studied was the Yale Art School website. The target audiences for
this website are most likely high school students who are interested in attending the Yale Art School, or
students who already attend the Yale Art School who are looking for information of some sort. The tasks I
was assigned were to find the Summer Programs tab, then find information about the beginner summer program,
and then find contact information on how to apply for this program.

Results

Overall, the webiste's usability is bad and the design of the website does not make it easy to easily find
or read information. To me this is kind of weird since it is the website for an art school that can be
edited by the students. The first task was the only one I was able to complete, where Summer Programs was
listed right in the navigation bar. After that, I was unable to find the beginner program information and
therefore I could not find the contact information or the application process either.
